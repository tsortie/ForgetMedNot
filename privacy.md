# Privacy Policy for ForgetMedNot

Last updated: [07/08/2026]

ForgetMedNot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any third party.

**What we store:**
All information — including your medicine log, reminder settings, and history — 
is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data is shared only between the ForgetMedNot 
app and its Home Screen Widget/Apple Watch app on your own device, using Apple's App Group feature, so that the app and widget 
stay in sync.

**What we don't do:**
- We do not collect any personal information
- We do not use analytics or tracking
- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not require an account or sign-in
- Your data never leaves your device

**Notifications:**
If you enable daily reminders, ForgetMedNot uses Apple's local notification system to schedule reminders on your device. 
No notification data is sent to us or anyone else.
